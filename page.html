Dear Bianca, who I have the pleasure to call ‘Meu amor’.
I decided to write this letter differently than the others because we have a special story love a bit different than the many stories. I want to express the feelings that I have for you, feelings of joy, thankless, love, satisfaction, contentment, happiness, and many others. I want to thank you for any happiness we have, any pain supported, any excited memories lived together, and beautiful dreams shared.

I love you too much and I have sure you know it, with you, I feel beauty in a sort of shapes, I learned how a person would be very special in my life in diverse forms, and observed how love would fulfill our lives in many different ways.
Since you came into my life, I had lived in paradise into me. When we’re together, time flows fast and froze at the same time, I feel that I’m too much close even though I am far.
I look at my past and I see that I did many changes, many choices, and many beginnings, these things make me know you, that’s I can be more proud of the things I lived.
My love, let’s remind some beautiful memories of our history, they are a treasure that we have, and celebrate how many happy we are together.
